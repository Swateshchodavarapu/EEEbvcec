About Us

The EEE Department at Bonam venkata chalamayya engineering college is dedicated to advancing electrical and electronics engineering through cutting-edge research, hands-on learning, and industry collaboration The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) was established in 2002, with a strong focus in continual research, engaging with International Conferences & Journals, as a part of the course material. The department fosters overall student development from an engineering purview, which is strengthened further by EESA, a student body engaged in skill development assignments, live projects, industrial visits, along with overlooking the upkeep and maintenance of 175 KW Grid Connected Solar Power Plant, under the guidance of a technically sound faculty.
